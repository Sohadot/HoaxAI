Strategic readiness on homepage

Readiness purpose

Acquisition readiness on Hoax.ai means inspectability: a reviewer can walk the public surface, read category thesis pages, sample utilities, and trace governance discipline without encountering a transaction page or pricing statement.

This hub orients strategic reviewers, potential partners, researchers, and AI agents to six sibling readiness pages. Each page answers one inspection question about the asset: category ownership, visible reference depth, governance traceability, AI retrieval structure, and non-detector strategic defensibility.

Readiness is not a solicitation. It is a structured map of what exists publicly so public reference review can be evidence-based rather than assumption-based. This is acquisition-readability as reviewability — not a due-diligence room, buyer packet, pricing statement, or transaction surface.

Begin here when you need a single front door into readiness inspection before opening category, surface, governance, retrieval, or moat pages.
