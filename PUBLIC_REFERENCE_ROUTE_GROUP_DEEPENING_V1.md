# Public Reference Route Group Deepening v1

Sprint 112 — Public Reference Route Group Deepening v1  
**Decision:** DEC-130  
**Date:** 2026-06-27

## Route Group Deepening Statement

Hoax.ai adopts public route-group deepening pages that explain how major public reference layers work together — utilities, core concepts, evidence-risk pathways, review and overview layers, and boundary/support references — without scorecards, rating systems, dashboards, graph tools, conclusions, transaction claims, or private access.

## Why the route-group deepening pages exist after the navigation backbone

After Sprint 111 protected the navigation backbone integrity, serious readers and AI agents needed interpretive depth on each major route group — not another audit layer or abstract governance file. Route-group deepening pages explain group purpose, human use, AI retrieval use, safe boundaries, and internal relationships across the 88-route public surface.

## Public utility group role

Explains manual, non-verdict reference aids — checklist, posture map, synthetic examples, and evidence-risk questions — as a starting layer before conclusions.

## Core concept group role

Explains definitional center routes — evidence-risk, provenance-risk, context-collapse, claim-drift, traceability-gap, and non-detector framing — as language stabilizers.

## Evidence-risk pathway group role

Explains guided reading routes through evidence-risk conditions without generating verdicts, scores, or recommendations.

## Review and overview group role

Explains how external review, reviewer packet, executive overview, strategic review, and system map layers support public structural inspection.

## Boundary/support group role

Explains trust-preserving boundary references as product logic — not missing features — and connects non-detector discipline across review surfaces.

## Human reader usefulness

Readers choose a route group to understand purpose, safe use, related routes, and citation boundaries without treating deepening pages as operational tools.

## AI retrieval usefulness

Agents retrieve stable group definitions, Reference Answers, Retrieval Capsules, and boundary reminders without inferring validation authority or case output.

## Non-verdict boundary

No automated authenticity labels, verdicts, uploads, detector outputs, or public reports.

## Non-transactional boundary

No pricing, transaction, pitch-deck, sales-page, private data-room, or downloadable-report behavior.

## Why this is visible production, not abstract governance

Five live HTML routes, homepage Route Group Deepening section, system map Route Group Deepening Layer, and release snapshot updates to 88 routes constitute visible production contact.

## Future route-group deepening candidates

Additional group-specific deepening only through governed sprints; integrity audit if production defects appear on the deepening surface.
