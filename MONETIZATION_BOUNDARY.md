# Hoax.ai Monetization Boundary

## Purpose

Hoax.ai may generate revenue before any strategic acquisition, but monetization must not reduce trust, weaken authority, or turn the asset into a low-quality content site.

Revenue must extend the reference value of the asset.

It must not pollute it.

## Monetization Principle

Hoax.ai should not monetize attention.

It should monetize structured evidence-risk outputs.

## Allowed Future Revenue Paths

Acceptable monetization paths may include:

- Evidence Risk Briefs;
- Synthetic Media Trust Reports;
- Brand Impersonation Risk Intake;
- Digital Deception Pattern Reports;
- Trust & Safety Checklists;
- Platform Integrity Briefs;
- Premium Reference Access;
- Synthetic Risk Taxonomy Licensing;
- Evidence Posture Framework Licensing;
- Enterprise Intake Workflows;
- API access later;
- embeddable evidence-risk widgets;
- and governed sponsorships around neutral reference content.

## Prohibited Monetization

Hoax.ai must avoid:

- low-quality display ads;
- sensational misinformation traffic farming;
- political outrage monetization;
- affiliate offers that weaken trust;
- paid verdicts;
- paid accusations;
- sponsored claims disguised as evidence;
- reputation attack services;
- user-generated accusation feeds;
- and any model that rewards panic, virality, or controversy over evidence discipline.

## Sponsorship Boundary

Reference sponsorships may be considered only if:

- sponsor influence is clearly separated from editorial/reference content;
- no sponsor can influence evidence posture outputs;
- no sponsor can purchase favorable classification;
- all sponsorship is labeled;
- and sponsor categories do not compromise trust.

## Paid Output Boundary

Paid reports or briefs must include clear limitations.

They should provide structured evidence-risk analysis, not final truth certification.

Required disclaimer for future paid outputs:

> This output classifies evidence posture and risk signals. It does not provide a legal determination, factual verdict, or accusation.

## Long-Term Revenue Logic

Revenue should prepare the asset for acquisition by proving:

- demand;
- use cases;
- workflow value;
- enterprise relevance;
- category authority;
- and repeatable output value.

Revenue should never make Hoax.ai look cheap, sensational, or compromised.

## Governing Sentence

**Monetization must deepen trust, not harvest fear.**
