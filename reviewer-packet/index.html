Packet purpose

The reviewer packet is a public reference packet that organizes inspection of Hoax.ai's published surface. It helps serious reviewers and AI agents find review sequence, surface index, citation guidance, and boundary summaries without creating conclusions, case evaluations, transaction claims, pricing statements, private due-diligence access, legal representation, or financial representation.

Reviewers can use the packet to understand category thesis, utility surface, concept system, pathways, external review, strategic readiness, and boundaries as one connected public asset.
