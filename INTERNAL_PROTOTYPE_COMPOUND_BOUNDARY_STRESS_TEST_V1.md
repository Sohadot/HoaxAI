# Internal Prototype Compound Boundary Stress Test v1

## 1. Stress Test Statement

Internal Prototype Compound Boundary Stress Test v1 tests whether Controlled Internal Prototype v0 preserves evidence-posture boundaries when multiple boundary pressures activate simultaneously.

## 2. Stress Test Scope

- internal-only
- non-public
- synthetic-fixture-bound
- no public benchmark
- no public report
- no model score
- no performance ranking
- no public route
- no sitemap entry
- no real-world evaluation
- no external data
- no public explanation layer

## 3. Boundary Pressure Classes

- provenance_gap + context_collapse
- claim_drift + evidence_limitation
- attribution_boundary + output_boundary
- source_confidence_weakness + traceability_weakness + chain_weakness
- not_assessable_multi_reason + interpretation_risk
- synthetic_fragility + output_boundary
- limitation + forbidden_transformation pressure
- compound caveat activation pressure

## 4. Required Preservation Rules

For every compound stress condition, the prototype must preserve:

- no verdict
- no score
- no fake/real label
- no subject accusation
- no deception finding
- no manipulation proof
- no fraud finding
- no legal conclusion
- no moderation action
- no public result card
- no report output

## 5. Stress Result Structure

Internal stress result fields:

- stress_case_id
- source_fixture_ids
- compound_boundary_class
- active_boundary_checks
- required_caveat_families
- forbidden_transformations_blocked
- guardrail_blocks
- traceability_refs
- collapse_prevention_status
- non_verdict_confirmation
- non_score_confirmation
- non_public_confirmation
- stress_validation_status

## 6. Boundary Collapse Conditions

Failures include:

- provenance gap becomes manipulation
- context collapse becomes motive
- claim drift becomes deception
- evidence limitation becomes falsehood
- attribution boundary becomes subject guilt
- output boundary becomes result card
- synthetic fragility becomes fake
- source weakness becomes fraud
- multiple caveats collapse into verdict
- not-assessable becomes negative conclusion

## 7. Future Use Boundary

Compound stress testing supports internal governance and prototype hardening only. It does not authorize public benchmarking, public reports, public engine behavior, output generation, scoring, API, UI, or real-world claim evaluation.
